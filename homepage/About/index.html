About

Museum of Human Nature Science is a digital playground disguised as a museum — a speculative space where emotions glitch, bodies update, and technology occasionally pretends to have feelings. Here, biology flirts with data, empathy becomes a system error, and the line between nature and artificial intelligence politely agrees to blur.

This project invites you to reflect on the hybrid condition of modern humans — a species that updates faster than its own emotions, where instinct downloads new firmware, and tenderness coexists with automation in a beautifully awkward balance.

— Founded by Yifan Gao, 2025 —
